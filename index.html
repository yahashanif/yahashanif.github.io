About Me

Hi! I'm Hanif, a dedicated Flutter developer with over 2 years of experience in building mobile applications. I specialize in developing Hospital Information System (HIS) version 3 using Go (Golang) for the backend and Eclipse BIRT for advanced reporting solutions. My focus is on creating reliable, scalable, and user-friendly mobile apps that enhance healthcare workflows and improve patient–doctor interactions.

My journey started directly with Flutter, where I discovered the power of building cross-platform applications efficiently without compromising on design. I enjoy solving complex problems and transforming ideas into reliable, user-friendly, and impactful mobile applications.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or learning about the latest mobile development technologies.
